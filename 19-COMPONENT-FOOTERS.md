# COMPONENT: ZFOOTER

## 1. VARIANTS
- `default`: Simple footer.
- `multi-column`: Multi-column footer.
- `minimal`: Minimal footer.

## 2. STRUCTURE
- `Links`: Quick links, legal, contact.
- `Social`: Social media icons.
- `Copyright`: Copyright notice.

## 3. RULES
- No locale/time strips.
- No version footers.
- Clean, simple layout.
