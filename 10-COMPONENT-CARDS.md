# COMPONENT: ZCARD

## 1. VARIANTS
- `default`: `border border-neutral-200 rounded-xl bg-white dark:bg-neutral-800`.
- `elevated`: `shadow-md hover:shadow-lg`.
- `interactive`: `cursor-pointer hover:border-accent-tech`.
- `warning`: `border-l-4 border-accent-warning bg-orange-50 dark:bg-orange-900/20`.
- `success`: `border-l-4 border-accent-success bg-green-50 dark:bg-green-900/20`.

## 2. STRUCTURE
- `Header`: Title + Actions.
- `Body`: Content, data, charts.
- `Footer`: CTAs, meta info.

## 3. RULES
- No empty cells in bento grids.
- No generic cards without content.
- Cards used only for hierarchy, not decoration.
- Max 2 lines headline, 20 words subtext.
