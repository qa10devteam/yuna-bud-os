# APP KOSZTORYS FEATURES

## 1. SPLIT VIEW
- Left: Document cost.
- Right: Your cost.
- Center: Difference.

## 2. TABLE
- Items list.
- Columns: Item, Doc Cost, Your Cost, Diff.
- Sortable, filterable.

## 3. SLIDERS
- Labor rate.
- Margin %.
- Material price.
- Efficiency %.
- Real-time update.

## 4. SUMMARY
- Total difference.
- Profit margin %.
- Risk level.
- "Go to Analysis" button.
