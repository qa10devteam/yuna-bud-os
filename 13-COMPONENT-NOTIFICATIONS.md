# COMPONENT: ZNOTIFICATION

## 1. VARIANTS
- `success`: Green left border, check icon.
- `warning`: Orange left border, warning icon.
- `error`: Red left border, error icon.
- `info`: Blue left border, info icon.

## 2. POSITION
- Top-right corner.
- Stack vertically.
- Auto-dismiss after 5s (optional).

## 3. RULES
- Notifications must be actionable.
- Close button always visible.
- No more than 3 visible at once.
