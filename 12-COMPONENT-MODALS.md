# COMPONENT: ZMODAL

## 1. VARIANTS
- `default`: Centered modal with backdrop.
- `side`: Slide-in from right (details panel).
- `fullscreen`: Full screen (forms, wizards).

## 2. STRUCTURE
- `Header`: Title + Close button.
- `Body`: Content, forms, data.
- `Footer`: Actions (Cancel, Confirm).

## 3. RULES
- Backdrop click to close (optional).
- Escape key to close.
- Focus trap inside modal.
- Scrollable body if content overflows.
